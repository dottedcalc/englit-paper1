Terms of service

  1. This tool only judges a full analytical essay based on an unseen literary passage (text-only; no multimedia). English A Language & Literature uses different expectations from Literature Paper 1; this workflow is not claiming to match Lang & Lit standards. Scoring is referenced to IB English A Paper 1 criteria. You may use it for other tests, but scores are not norm-referenced to those examinations.
  2. International Baccalaureate and IB are trademarks of the International Baccalaureate Organization. This site and tool are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the IBO or any IB school.
  3. Essay grading is inherently subjective. We use a proprietary prompt and multi-step workflow, but model outputs involve randomness and may differ from your teacher’s mark. No refunds will be issued for disagreement with an instructor’s grade or for perceived inaccuracy of scores shown here.
  4. You must supply your own OpenRouter API key and choose a supported model in the section below. Usage is billed by OpenRouter (and reflected on your OpenRouter account) according to the model’s listed pricing. Different models will change scoring: more advanced, sophisticated models tend to produce more consistent, accurate judgments than smaller or lighter models, though no model guarantees a match to an official IB mark.
  5. Your API key, source text, student response, and saved criterion results are kept only in this browser (e.g. localStorage); they are not uploaded to or stored on this site’s servers. Model responses are not used to train any model owned or operated by the developer. By using the tool you consent to sending your inputs (and prompts built from them) to OpenRouter, which routes requests to the model host you select, under OpenRouter’s terms and the upstream provider’s policies where applicable.
  6. Do not refresh the page while grading or during an active session—you might lose progress. Although this tool tries to persist state in this browser, a refresh or navigation is not guaranteed to preserve your work.

Model API

All requests use OpenRouter (/api/v1/chat/completions). Each option is an OpenRouter model id. Use an OpenRouter API key (sk-or-v1-…). Your key and model choice are stored in this browser (localStorage). See the Terms of service for liability.
